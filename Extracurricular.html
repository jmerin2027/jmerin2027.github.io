Extracurricular Activities


Leadership Work and Positions

When joining different clubs and events, you may find yourself in a position where you have to lead a group. Your skills in Organization, Communication, Empathy, and Strategic thinking will be tested and developed during the activities.

Artistic and Creative Pursuits

We encourage student’s artistic expressions. Art is an important way to communicate a student’s ideas and feelings. Whether it be music, dancing, writing, or drawing, any creative outlet is important for a student’s mental well-being.

Sports and Athletic Participation

Sports are essential for students to be mentally and physically healthy. Helping students exercise teamwork, patience, and confidence. Reducing stress while teaching students how to manage their time and balance their activities.

Academic Clubs and Teams

Various clubs are available for students to join to help them discover new interests and talents. Students are able to engage with their other peers in the campus and make new friends in the clubs they joined in.

Volunteering and Community Services

To develop a different perspective and to see how their activities impacts the lives of others. Volunteering helps them become more open-minded and connect what they learn in school to real life.
