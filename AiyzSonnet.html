Certainly, you were in the right
All those before you were slain
Yet, it is apparent your power is in vain

Left in perpetual disdain;
Unending, torturous restraint.

The unending conflict of your mind
Interferes in your way of thought…

Even with your mental fortitude waning,
There can be no compromise,
This inclement clemency fraught.
